About Sunny Staffing,

We Provide all Kinds of Jobs and Manpower

As a staffing agency in Montreal, Prabhjot Singh assists companies of all sizes with finding highly skilled finance, accounting, administrative and technology professionals and labour or daily wages workers . Our dedicated recruiters typically have extensive industry experience in the fields they serve, giving them unparalleled ability to understand the temporary, temporary-to-hire, project and full-time staffing requirements specific to your business. Employment agencies are experts at placing the right people in the right positions. But administrative tasks such as payroll, financing and government compliance can slow a small business down and keep them from focusing on what they do best.The Staffing Edge’s services are bolstered by the strength of its staff. The company hires employees with prior experience in the staffing industry to better address the needs of its clients.

Our Vision & Values

Where we stand in the world of work. No matter where we are or what we do, there are fundamental beliefs and behaviours that guide our decisions, focus our actions and unite us as a company. Our vision and values serve to inspire how we interact, create and deliver on our promise to clients, job seekers and ourselves.
Our vision is to lead in the creation and delivery of innovative workforce solutions and services that enable our clients to win in the changing world of work. We care about people and the role of work in their lives. We respect people as individuals, trusting them, supporting them, enabling them to achieve their aims in work and in life. We help people develop their careers through planning, work, coaching and training. We recognize everyone's contribution to our success – our people, our clients and our candidates. We encourage and reward achievement.
